From Passion to Precision::
Meet the Designer Behind
Starburst Creative

Hi, I’m Michelle Williams, the creative force behind Starburst Creative. My design journey began at 17 when I sold my first design through a high school logo contest, sparking a lifelong passion for creativity. With a deep love for color theory, fine art, and illustration, I’ve spent decades refining my craft through continuous learning and exploration.

Drawing inspiration from vintage aesthetics, high fashion, and the beauty industry, my work balances timeless elegance with modern appeal. I’ve had the privilege of designing for a variety of industries, including medicine, music, real estate, and automotive, bringing unique perspectives to each project. At Starburst Creative, every design is a collaboration—crafted with care, creativity, and a focus on making your brand unforgettable.
